FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to the questions most students ask before enrolling.

Are the programs online or in-person?▼
Both options are available. In-person sessions are held at our training centre, and online programs are conducted via live sessions with recordings available. Contact us to discuss which format suits you.
What software will I need?▼
For CAD programs, you will need access to SolidWorks, CATIA V5, or Creo. We will guide you on obtaining student licenses or trial versions. For other programs, standard tools like MS Office are sufficient.
Do I get a certificate at the end?▼
Yes — a MechTech Careers completion certificate is issued for each program. More importantly, you will leave with a portfolio of real work that is far more valuable in interviews than a certificate alone.
Can I enroll in multiple programs?▼
Absolutely — many students combine programs like Mechanical Design + CAD Professional, or Manufacturing + Quality. We can create a custom learning path based on your goals. Just mention it in your enquiry.
What if I am already working?▼
Programs are designed to be flexible for working professionals. Weekend batches and evening sessions are available. Many working engineers join to qualify for senior roles or overseas opportunities.
How is this different from other CAD institutes?▼
Most training institutes teach software. MechTech teaches how industry actually uses that software — with real design intent, OEM-level workflows, and a founder who spent decades doing this at Volvo and Force Motors. The difference shows up in interviews.
